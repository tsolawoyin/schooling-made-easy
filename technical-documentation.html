Please follow the steps below to get your account up and running on your mobile device

  1. If you have Discord already installed please click here to join us now, but if not, follow the steps below
  2. To get Discord on your device click here.
  3. Click the Sign up for Discord or preferably, click Download from Google Play or other app store based on your device.
  4. After installing the app, click the Register button
  5. You will be prompted to register using your phone number or email, feel free to choose anyone you like.
  6. You will be prompted to verify by hCaptcha, click the verify button. Note, this is just to prove you are a human.
  7. Select the relevant images and click next.
  8. If you choose to register with phone number, you will likely be sent a confirmation code, fill in the numbers sent to you. In case you don't get any confirmation code, click the resend button, and if persistent, you can go back and regsiter with email instead. Be sure to double check if the number you provided is correct. Thank you
  9. After confirmation, please enter your name and other personal information.
  10. Now you are almost done. You may have the image below in front of you click the Join a friend on discord button then click or copy and paste the following https://discord.gg/JttcmZz8FM into the input field provided. Click the join button and hurray! Welcome to our community.
  11. If you are still having any issues, it's fine, please send us a direct message by visiting our contact-us And you may click here to watch our tutorial on how to create an account with discord on Youtube. Thank you.
